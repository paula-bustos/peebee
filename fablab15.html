WEEK 15.

INTERFACE AND APPLICATION PROGRAMMING.

May 18, 2022

In this week we decided to keep developing our termometer for the solar oven. Our intention is to display the temperature in a visual way so the user of the oven can tell which is the temperature not only with the leds but also with the platform. Ideally we would like to make it a platform in which we can collect this data and study further the efficiency of the solar ovens. We can check the time and temperature needed to cook our food and also make a community around it in which they can exchange tips and comments around better use of the solar ovens. We started by making the code for the leds connected to the temperature levels and the start and stop button.

After testing the code and being succesful with it we passed it to p5js to make it visual.
