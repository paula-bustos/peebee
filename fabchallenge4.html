FAB CHALLENGE.

MICRO CHALLENGE 4.

June 5, 2022

"Integrated prototype” that helps your MDEF Project /Fest and integrates a range of units covered.

In this microchallenge I continued to work with the SlowLab. We are planning an event for the MDEFest in which we do a SolarBrunch and show some low-techs we've been working on. We also want to show people how they can integrate these low-techs into their everyday lives. So with these 3 prototypes that we're building from the challenge we can implement a station for people to have at home and have alternative energy systems. The 3 things we're building are a rotatory base that follows the sunlight so we can put the solar oven on top or a plant, photobioreactor or anything that needs sunlight. We're also building to bike energy generators. One small one to carry while you're biking outdoors and can charge your phone or be connected to anything that uses USB. The big one is for a stationary bike and can power anything with an input of 20v. You can check our repo in here and read our miro board here.
