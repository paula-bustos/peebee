FAB CHALLENGE.

MICRO CHALLENGE 2.

April 13, 2022

Develop an artifact “that allows you to explain your project to others” or “interact with others so that they understand your research project.”

In this microchallenge I decided to collaborate with Marina, Pippa and Paula del Rio. We have similar interests in aiding the growth of species and creating an interspecies collaboration. We all want to approach algae and understand what it needs to grow in order to then be able to create somwthing else with it. We decided we wanted to design a photobioreactor so we could understand this better and have a place in which we would be growing this species. We made a repo with all the info about the project which you can check in HERE.
